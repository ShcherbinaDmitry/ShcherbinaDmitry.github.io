Foundation of Magnitogorsk

Magnitogorsk was founded in 1743 as part of the Orenburg Line of forts built during the reign of the Empress Elizabeth

City's planning

According to the original plans, the city was to have followed the linear city design, with rows of similar superblock neighborhoods running parallel to the factory, with a strip of greenery, or greenbelt, separating them.

After USSR

During perestroika the closed-city status was removed and foreigners were allowed to visit the city again. The years after perestroika brought a significant change in the life of the city.
